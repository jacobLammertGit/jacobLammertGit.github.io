About Me

My name is Jacob Lammert and I am a Front-End Web developer. My coding journey started at the end of 2020. When searching for a new career path, I discovered web development. I never thought coding would interest me, but after doing more research, I quickly saw myself doing this for a career. I loved the opportunities it would allow me to have in the future. I have always loved being able to get creative and the front-end is where I feel most comfortable. After learning HTML and CSS, I went back to school and got my Computer Science Associates Degree. From there, I decided to self teach the rest of the way using resources like Udemy, Youtube, freecodecamp, and of course, Google. I now focus on building personal projects for my portfolio to practice my coding skills and build a stronger portfolio.
